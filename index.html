our little story

Born from cravings,
creativity, and a
fluffy “not‑a‑cat.”

✦

Meowchi started as a simple student project by an FEU–Manila student with a very clear goal: to get an A in her Professional Selling subject—and hopefully earn enough money to buy a pickleball paddle. The capital used to start the business was actually the money saved for that paddle, which made the plan straightforward: sell products, earn it back, and end the business once the goal was reached.

The idea was originally palitaw bites, but being a student who constantly craved mochi—and couldn’t find it easily or affordably in her city—the concept slowly changed. Instead of waiting for mochi to appear, she decided to create her own and share that craving with others. That decision led to the birth of Meowchi.

Meowchi was never meant to last. It was supposed to be a one‑time business, just enough to recover the capital and complete a school requirement. But as the process unfolded—branding the business, creating characters, and building stories behind the name—the owner slowly fell in love with it. What started as an academic requirement turned into a passion project.

Despite the cat‑themed name, the owner doesn’t actually have a cat. She has a lively Pomeranian whose pointy ears and fluffy appearance often make people mistake it for a little cat. That playful confusion ended up fitting perfectly with the brand’s cute and whimsical personality.
Meowchi became more than just a way to earn back money. It became a brand worth continuing—born from cravings, creativity, and a fluffy “not‑a‑cat” companion.

Meowchi is made with care, curiosity, and a lot of love for soft, comforting things. It started as a small student project, but it slowly grew into something I truly enjoy creating.

Every piece is meant to feel warm, familiar, and a little special—something to enjoy in between busy days. I keep Meowchi small and personal, and I make each batch with the same intention: comfort, softness, and joy in every bite.

— Abby, founder
