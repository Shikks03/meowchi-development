our little story

A tiny kitchen
with three cats
and a dream.

Meowchi started in a second-floor apartment with one rice cooker, a very patient roommate, and a running joke about which of the three cats each flavor looked most like. The joke stuck. The cats named themselves. The mochi kept disappearing from the counter.

Every daifuku is still folded by hand in small batches — no factory, no preservatives, no rush. We source glutinous rice from a single mill, strawberries from the farmer up the road, and matcha flown in from Uji. The chocolate? That's a secret. (Ask Chat.)

We make mochi the way our cats nap: slowly, deliberately, and with complete disregard for reasonable hours.

owner's note

To make every
box feel like
a tiny hug.

Our goal is simple: I want the moment you open a Meowchi box to feel the way my kitchen feels at 6am — warm rice, warm light, three cats politely asking what's for breakfast.

We won't scale past what our hands can fold. We'll always answer your emails. And we'll keep inventing characters until the whole shelf feels like a little neighborhood.

— Abby, founder & head squisher
